Website and Application's Features

Wird project provides alot of options which helps supervisors and students to improve there Ramadan performance

A website for educators and supervisors, where they arrange the standards of their contest, and monitor indivial and group achievements


Supervisors can use the website to
  • Create and adjust their contest standarts
  • Monitor rhe performance of students and kids through diagrams and tables
  • Split students into groups and assign a supervisor for each group
  • Create advertisments on a public or a private level

An application for our kids and students to register their dailt information and compete with others


Students can use the application to
  • Recording daily devotional data
  • Follow-up on the student's own performance through graphs and daily performance tables
  • Compete with other contest participants
